About SignFlow

Our SignFlow: Real-Time Sign Language Translation system bridges the communication gap between sign language users and the wider community using AI-powered gesture recognition. The system translates Indian Sign Language (ISL) gestures into text and speech in real-time, making interactions more inclusive and accessible. Additionally, it converts text and speech into corresponding sign language gestures, enabling a seamless two-way communication experience. Designed with advanced computer vision and deep learning models, our platform empowers individuals with hearing or speech impairments by providing an intuitive and efficient way to communicate effortlessly.

Key Features

Real-Time Sign Recognition

Accurately detects and translates ISL gestures into text and speech using AI-powered computer vision.

Two-Way Communication

Converts both sign language to text/speech and text/speech to sign gestures for seamless interaction.

Multi-platform Support

Access SignFlow seamlessly across all your devices with perfect synchronization and consistent performance.

Speech Output for Recognized Signs

Converts detected gestures into spoken words using text-to-speech (TTS) for enhanced communication.

User Feedback System

Includes a feedback form to collect user responses and improve accuracy and usability over time.
